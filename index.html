About

Building Bridges Across Continents

Venkkat Prannit Nissankara is a Munich-based community leader and entrepreneur with extensive experience in cultural diplomacy, social integration, and public engagement. As the founder of IndoEuropean.eu, he has built one of the largest networking platforms for Indians living in Europe.

Guided by the philosophy of Vasudeva Kutumbakam (The World is One Family), his work bridges geographical gaps and fosters a deep sense of belonging among non-resident Indians across the continent.

At just 28, he became the Founder President of the Mana Telugu Association, successfully consolidating Telugu community platforms throughout Germany. His contributions were recognized with the prestigious Under-40 Award at the Belgian Parliament.
